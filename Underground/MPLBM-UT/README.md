# MPLBM-UT

以格子Boltzmann方法的palabo库为求解后端，使用CT扫描的岩石样品的图像数据为输入，模拟孔隙流动下的毛细压力与相对渗透率等的物理过程

MPI集群并行

编程语言： FORTRAN

## 参考文献

Javier E. Santos, et al. MPLBM-UT: Multiphase LBM library for permeable media analysis. SoftwareX 18 (2022) 101097

Jonas Latt, et al. Palabos: Parallel Lattice Boltzmann Solver. Computers and Mathematics with Applications 81 (2021) 334C350

Xiaoyu Wang, Liang Gong, Yang Li, Jun Yao. 2023. Developments and applications of the CFD-DEM method in particleCfluid numerical simulation in petroleum engineering: A review. Applied Thermal Engineering, 222(5), 119865
